# Monster Stat Blocks

## Goblerkin Warrior
*Medium humanoid, neutral evil*

---

**Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)

**Hit Points** 22 (5d8+2)

**Speed** 30 ft.

---

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 8 (-1) |

---

**Challenge** 1

### Traits

***Feather Step.*** The Goblerkin can use its action to create a 10-foot square of difficult terrain as feathers explode from its body.

***Gobble Charge.*** If the Goblerkin moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a melee attack on the same turn, the target must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

### Actions

***Beak Lash.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6+3) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) psychic damage as the Goblerkin's beak latches onto the target's mind.

***Feather Barrage.*** Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (2d6) piercing damage as the Goblerkin throws a handful of enchanted feathers.

---

## Ice Wyrm
*Large dragon, chaotic evil*

---

**Armor Class** 17 (natural armor)

**Hit Points** 85 (10d10+30)

**Speed** 30 ft., swim 30 ft.

---

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 8 (-1) |

---

**Challenge** 5

### Traits

***Ice Camouflage.*** The Ice Wyrm has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in icy or snowy terrain.

***Cold Blooded.*** The Ice Wyrm has resistance to cold damage.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The Ice Wyrm makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its tail.

***Bite.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d10+4) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) cold damage.

***Tail.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d8+4) bludgeoning damage.

***Freezing Breath (Recharge 5-6).*** The Ice Wyrm exhales a 30-foot line of freezing mist. Each creature in the line must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 35 (10d6) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

---

## Gorm the Frost Giant
*Huge giant, lawful evil*

---

**Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)

**Hit Points** 138 (16d12+48)

**Speed** 30 ft.

---

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 23 (+6) | 10 (+0) | 18 (+4) | 10 (+0) | 12 (+1) | 12 (+1) |

---

**Challenge** 7

### Traits

***Keen Smell.*** The giant has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

***Iceborn.*** The giant has resistance to cold damage.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The giant makes two greatclub attacks.

***Greatclub.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (3d8+6) bludgeoning damage.

***Frostforged Club (Recharge 5-6).*** The giant's frostforged club crackles with icy energy. The giant makes one greatclub attack. On a hit, the target must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 14 (4d6) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The target is also grappled (escape DC 14) and restrained until it uses an action to break free.
