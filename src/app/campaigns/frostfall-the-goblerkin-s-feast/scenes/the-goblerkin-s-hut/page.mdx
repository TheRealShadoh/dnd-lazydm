# The Goblerkin's Hut

> *Before you stands a hut like no otherâ€”a grotesque amalgamation of stolen armor, enchanted feathers, and twisted wood. The structure seems to breathe, its walls shifting and groaning as if alive. A low, rhythmic chanting drifts from within, accompanied by the clatter of plates and the sizzle of cooking meat. The air is thick with the scent of roasting fowl, and a warm, orange glow spills from the cracks in the walls. You can't help but feel a mix of curiosity and dread.*

The party arrives at the Goblerkin's fortified hut, a bizarre structure made of stolen armor and enchanted feathers. They must navigate the complex social dynamics of the Goblerkin and uncover the truth behind the disappearances.

## Objectives

- Gain the Goblerkin's trust or infiltrate their hut to learn the truth about the missing hunters.
- Find evidence of the disappearances or rescue any survivors.
- Decide whether to confront the Goblerkin or leave peacefully.

## NPCs

### Magda the Matriarch
**Role:** Host and de facto leader of the Goblerkin

She will try to manipulate the party, offering them a place at her table while secretly plotting their doom.

### Clucklow the Cautious
**Role:** Potential ally and informant

She will try to help the party if they prove themselves trustworthy, but she is afraid of Magda's wrath.

## Encounters

### The Goblerkin's Feast
**Difficulty:** medium

The party is invited to a feast where they must navigate the Goblerkin's eccentric hospitality and uncover the dark truth beneath the surface.

### Confrontation with the Goblerkin
**Difficulty:** hard

If the party confronts the Goblerkin, they must battle their way through the feast and escape the hut.

**Monsters:**
- 4x Goblerkin Warrior
- 1x Magda the Matriarch


## Treasure

- A chest filled with stolen armor and trinkets.
- A magical turkey feather that grants temporary flight once per day.

## DM Secrets

- The Goblerkin are not true humanoids but the result of a curse that transformed a tribe of turkey-herders.
- Magda is using the missing hunters as ingredients in her eternal feast.
- Clucklow has been hiding a wounded hunter in the hut's cellar.
