# The Frozen Lake

> *The frozen lake stretches out before you, its surface a patchwork of ice and snow. The wind whips across the expanse, carrying with it the distant howls of unseen creatures. The ice groans and cracks beneath your feet, and you can't help but feel that it's only a matter of time before it gives way. In the distance, the silhouette of a massive figure loomsâ€”a guardian of the lake, or perhaps a predator waiting for its next meal.*

The party must cross a treacherous frozen lake to escape the Goblerkin or reach Gorm the Frost Giant's lair. The lake is home to ice wyrms and other dangers.

## Objectives

- Cross the frozen lake safely and reach the other side.
- Avoid or defeat the dangers lurking beneath the ice.
- Decide whether to confront Gorm the Frost Giant or search for another path.

## NPCs

### Gorm the Frost Giant
**Role:** Guardian and predator of the frozen lake

He will see the party as intruders and attack on sight, but he may be persuaded to let them pass if they prove their strength.

## Encounters

### Ice Wyrm Ambush
**Difficulty:** hard

An ice wyrm bursts from the frozen lake, attacking the party as they attempt to cross.

**Monsters:**
- 1x Ice Wyrm

### Confrontation with Gorm
**Difficulty:** deadly

If the party approaches Gorm's lair, they must battle the frost giant for control of the lake.

**Monsters:**
- 1x Gorm the Frost Giant


## Treasure

- A chest filled with frozen gold and jewels.
- A magical amulet that allows the wearer to breathe underwater for a short time.

## DM Secrets

- The frozen lake is home to an ancient, slumbering ice wyrm that the Goblerkin and Gorm both fear.
- Gorm is secretly allied with the Goblerkin's rivals, the Ice Wyrms, and is using the lake as a battleground.
- The lake is enchanted, and those who cross it without permission may be cursed with eternal cold.
